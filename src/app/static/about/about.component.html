The Liquid investigations project is a Google DNI funded project, driven by the Romanian Centre for Investigative Journalism (CRJI), where coders and journalists work towards making investigative collaborations less burdensome and more secure.

If you think you have run into a problem or need support, you can contact our development and support team at contact@liquiddemo.org
